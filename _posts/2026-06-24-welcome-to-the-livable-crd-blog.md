Welcome to the Livable CRD blog. This is where our coalition will share what
we're working on between now and election day — in plain language, and out in
the open.

[Livable CRD](/about/) is a non-partisan voter-education project. We don't
endorse candidates; we publish a transparent [scorecard](/scorecard/) so
residents across the Capital Regional District can see where municipal
candidates stand on the issues that shape daily life: transit, housing, climate,
arts, walking, cycling, youth, healthcare, and general livability.

## What you'll find here

- **Scorecard progress** — updates as we survey candidates and publish their
  responses by municipality.
- **Methodology notes** — how we write questions, collect answers, and assign
  letter grades, so the process stays reproducible.
- **Ways to get involved** — calls for volunteers, partner organizations, and
  supporters.

## Where things stand

Participating organizations are drafting a shared questionnaire now. Once
candidates are surveyed, ratings will be published ahead of election day. We'll
post milestones here as they land.

Have a question, a correction, or want to get involved?
[Get in touch](/about/#contact) — and if you'd like to follow along, subscribe
to our [RSS feed](/feed.xml).
