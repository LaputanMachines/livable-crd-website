**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Victoria, BC, July 22, 2026** — A coalition of community organizations from
across the Capital Regional District today announced **Livable CRD**, a
non-partisan voter-education project that will publish a candidate scorecard for
the region's municipal elections. The scorecard grades candidates on the issues
that shape daily life across the region: transit, housing, arts, climate,
walking, cycling, youth, healthcare, and general livability.

Livable CRD does not endorse candidates. Instead, it publishes candidates'
positions in a clear, comparable format so residents can make informed choices
at the ballot box.

"Voters deserve a straightforward way to see where the people asking for their
vote actually stand," said a Livable CRD coalition spokesperson. "We are not
telling anyone how to vote. We are doing the legwork so that every resident can
compare candidates side by side, in plain language, before election day."

## How the scorecard works

Participating organizations collaborate on a shared questionnaire, with each
group contributing questions in its area of expertise. Candidates are surveyed,
and their responses are graded against a published, reproducible methodology
using a clear letter-grade system. Where a candidate declines to respond, that
is documented as well. Results will be organized by municipality so voters can
focus on the races on their own ballot.

## A growing coalition

Livable CRD brings together advocates from across the region, including Homes
for Living, Walk On Victoria, Better Island Transit, Climate Justice Victoria,
Family Doctors for Saanich, Better Transit YYJ, and Victori'us Arts. The
coalition continues to grow, and new member organizations are welcome to join.

## What comes next

The coalition is finalizing its questionnaire and methodology now. Candidate
surveys, per-municipality pages, and full candidate profiles with letter grades
will be published ahead of election day. Progress updates will appear on the
[Livable CRD blog](/blog/), and residents can
[subscribe to the mailing list](/signup/) to follow along.

## About Livable CRD

Livable CRD is a non-partisan coalition of community groups preparing a
candidate scorecard for municipal elections across the Capital Regional District
of British Columbia. The coalition informs; it does not endorse. Learn more at
[livablecrd.ca](/) or read more [about the coalition](/about/).
